System Prompt payment_prompt.py
System Prompt · identity, voice & contextGives the agent who it is (name & company), how it speaks, and the borrower's context. It's the agent's core knowledge — but a prompt alone can't govern the flow of a compliant call. That's what Layers 2 & 3 add.
Borrower
The BorrowerThe agent dials them — an outbound call. They answer, speak, and hear the agent's reply.
Payment Agentrun.py · worker
Payment Agent · orchestratorThe agent worker running on LiveKit. It dispatches the outbound call and drives the STT→LLM→TTS session.
TwilioPSTN · SIP
Twilio · telephonyElastic SIP trunk that carries the outbound call over the PSTN and supplies the caller ID.
Speech → Textdeepgram · whisper
STT · speech to textStreaming transcription — Deepgram nova-3 by default, OpenAI Whisper as an alternative, gated by Silero VAD.
LLMgpt-4o-mini · gemini
LLM · the reasoning coreReads the transcript and drafts the reply. Pluggable — OpenAI or Gemini. It can think and talk, but on its own it can't govern a compliant call.
Murftext → speech
Murf · text to speechTurns the reply text into natural-sounding speech in the agent's configured voice, sent back to the borrower.
